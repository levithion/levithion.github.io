03Deep recommendation2025

Neural Movie Recommender

A neural collaborative-filtering model that learns non-linear user–movie interactions from latent embeddings.

The repository implementation concatenates 64-dimensional user and movie embeddings, passes them through a 128→64→32 MLP, adds learned user/movie/global biases, and serves ranked predictions through FastAPI.
